About Me

I'm a biologist turned web developer! I discovered my passion for science and healthcare during my undergraduate career at Michigan State University. Some of my favorite courses that I took during that time were mechanisms of disease, molecular pathogenesis, physiology, neuroscience of learning, personality psychology, clinical immunology, and virology.


I loved those courses because I'm the type of person who truly enjoys understanding the components of complex systems and how those components interact with each other. The implications for that kind of understanding in the healthcare field are invaluable: remedies can be developed, symptoms can be mitigated, and lives can be saved. As someone who is equally as empathetic as I am detail oriented, I will always have a love for human health.


I have worked and volunteered in various roles, including as a behavioral neuroscience research assistant, a crisis counselor, a hospital volunteer, a transcriptionist, and an editor. Through those opportunities, I have recognized the importance of combining a thoughtful, scientific approach with empathy. I believe in the power of data and in the power of compassion, and I have seen the impact of each.


Over the course of 2021, I discovered a new love for web development and computer programming. For me, it incorporates the best of everything: a scientific, logical approach to problems, a need for discerning and resolving small errors, an opportunity to be creative, and a limitless potential to help those in need.


I've spent the past year developing my technical skills as a web developer. I received a certificate in full-stack web development from Michigan State University, and I have obtained over 10 other certifications relevant to building a stellar website with a strong digital presence. I'm seeking opportunities where I can continue to build upon those skills, and I would especially love to work for a company with a healthcare or scientific focus.


Below are a few web development projects I have created or worked with a team to create. For additional projects, feel free to visit my GitHub profile at the bottom of this page. Please also feel free to contact me with any professional inquiries.
